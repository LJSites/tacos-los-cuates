Who We Are

The real
deal trompo.

Tacos Los Cuates is Arcadia's late-night street taco spot — the kind of place where a spinning trompo of al pastor glows in the dark and the tortillas are pressed by hand to order. We set up on Peck Road every evening and don't close until the neighborhood has eaten well.

Our menu runs deep: carne asada, al pastor, buche, cabeza, chorizo, pollo, and more. Load your tray at the salsa bar — pico de gallo, cucumbers, radishes, multiple salsas — and wash it all down with a fresh agua fresca or horchata. Simple. Honest. Delicious.
